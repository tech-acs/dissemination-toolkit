# Announcements

Announcements are broadcast messages sent to all users of a selected role. Accessible at `manage/announcement`.

## Creating an announcement

1. Navigate to `manage/announcement`
2. Click **Create**
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Title** — the announcement subject
   - **Recipients** — select a role; all users with that role receive the notification
   - **Message** — the body of the announcement
4. Click **Send**

Once sent, the announcement appears in the recipients' notification inbox on the public site.
