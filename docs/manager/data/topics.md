# Topics

Topics organise indicators, visualisations, and data stories into semantic categories for easier browsing. Accessible at `manage/topic`.

## Creating a topic

1. Navigate to `manage/topic`
2. Click **Create**
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Name** (multi-language)
   - **Description** (multi-language)
   - **Code** — a unique identifier
   - **Rank** — display ordering
4. Click **Save**

## Editing a topic

1. Click **Edit** next to a topic in the list
2. Update the fields as needed
3. Click **Update**

## Deleting a topic

1. Click **Delete** next to a topic
2. Confirm in the dialog
